The idea

Wichita needs more affordable housing. You have a backyard.

We believe the solutions to our collective needs are found within our own communities. Wichita homeowners have unused outdoor space. Their neighbors need safe, affordable places to live. IMBY brings those two realities together.

You add an ADU to your backyard. A neighbor gets a quality home in an established neighborhood. You earn steady monthly income. Everyone wins.

We believe we don't have to let fear lead us. Putting good in your backyard is the right thing to do — and it makes financial sense too.
